About-Library

The college library has a total collection of 25,059 books, valuable reference books, journals, carrer guidance books and keeping a separate record of books of book bank.

Students are provided with a Reader's Card for reading room in the library and a Borrower's Card which enables them to take books on loan. These cards are non-transferable and must be returned at the end of the session. A general student may be issued two books at a time, one book from the Book bank for a maximum period of three months and one from the college library for a maximum period of 14 days. However an honours student may take three books one from the Book Bank for a maximum period of three months and two from the college library for a maximum period of 14 days. Detailed library rules are available in the library.

Recently the college library has implemented the Inflibnet N-LIST programme under the UGC INFONET Digital Library Consortium for accessing e-resources.
