Computer Center


The Computer Centre provides quality service to the University community in computing facilities, learning, teaching, training, Research, e-mailing, website hosting, Development and Management etc.

As well as the core IT systems, tools and services that keep the University running day-to-day and it helps researchers to get their valuable data analysed, extends Internet facilities in the University, enables access to World Wide Web and provides laboratory facility to the students of various courses. The centre caters the needs of the students, faculty and staff members of the University.
